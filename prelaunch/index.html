About

Tribal Live specialises in the production and streaming of live and on-demand video for online and mobile audiences. Utilizing the latest 360 video and IP workflow technology, Tribal Live produces large multi-camera shows, e-sports competitions, sophisticated web programs and immerse video experiences.

Situated at Fox Studios Australia, with its own purpose built, multi-camera live streaming studio and master control, Tribal Lives scale and investment in technology positions it as Australia’s premiere provider of production, streaming and distribution solutions for clients wanting to connect with audiences, build brands and unlock revenue opportunities through the production and distribution of live cross channel and social video experiences.

Live Streaming

Tribal Live provides complete multi-camera solutions for clients looking to live stream visually stunning programs across digital channels and social media platforms. Whether it is a TV show, concert, sporting event, e-sports competition, press conference, product launch, annual general meeting or corporate event, Tribal Live creates live video programs with limitless design, production and distribution possibilities.

Studio

Tribal Live’s purpose built live streaming studio and master control room is situated at Fox Studios Australia.

Equipped with six HD cameras, a Newtek Tricaster 8000 production system with NDI Live IP Workflow system, Live Link servers and 100Gbps fibre upload connectivity.

The studio is used to produce live talk show style programming and is Tribal Live’s master control room where live links can be received and switched before being distributed as a program to client CDN’s, channels and social media platforms.

Pre-Recorded Entertainment

Equipped with the most powerful multi-camera video production system on the market Tribal Lives’ studio and master control are the perfect solution for producing talk show styling programming and branded entertainment spots.

Situated at Fox Studios Australia, Tribal Lives’ master control room is equipped with an 8-M/E, 24 channel video switcher with an integrated audio mixer, medial players, titles, graphics and DVE effects. Router extensible, the Tribal Live’s production system has the power to produce big live shows and sporting events, which demand up to 40 HD cameras.

For publishers and high volume content creators, Tribal Live’s studio provides them with the resources and pipeline to cost effectively create premium content, which allows them to break stories, reach audiences first and capitalise on digital and social distribution and monetisation opportunities.

Distribution & Syndication

Tribal Live can simultaneously publish multi-camera live productions to any website and social media platforms including, YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, Twitter, Periscope, Microsoft Azure, U Stream, FTP, LinkedIn Imgur and Vid.me.

Tribal Live also implements syndication strategies for clients looking to distribute their video content beyond their own website and social media platforms. Providing deep syndication support, Tribal Live can implement open, viral and trusted party syndication strategies, as well as create custom branded, dedicated video players, for placement on third party websites.

Further helping clients maximise content monetisation opportunities, Tribal Live can work with clients program and create custom feeds for dedicated video player created as part of trusted party syndication strategies.

Branded Live Players

Monetisation

Broadcast Graphics

VR & AR Sets

Taking productions to the next level, Tribal Live can create custom, photorealistic virtual environments from panoramic images, with support for 3D positioning of talent and live camera movements.

Connectivity

Tribal Live can live stream events when there is no Internet or satellite connection by live linking the event back to Tribal Live’s master control room at Fox Studios Australia. From here, Tribal Live can distribute and syndicate live events to an endless number of channels and platforms simultaneously, including client CDN’s, Facebook, YouTube, YouTube Live, Twitter, Twitch, LinkedIn and UStream.

Live Links

Tribal Live can produce multi-camera live events even when from areas where connectivity is poor. Using bonded cellular uplink units, which link directly back to Tribal Lives’ master control room, Tribal Live can switch, produce and distribute multi-camera live events from Fox Studios Australia, without having to organise enormous onsite internet connections, OB trucks and crews.

When connectivity is poor and there is a requirement to simultaneously publish live streams to multiple platforms, the combined power of live links and Tribal Lives’ fibre redundant master control room is an invaluable asset.
