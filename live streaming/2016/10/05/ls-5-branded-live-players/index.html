About

Tribal Live specialises in the production and streaming of live and on-demand video for online and mobile audiences. Utilizing the latest IP workflow technology, Tribal Live produces multi-camera shows, sophisticated web programs and immersive video experiences for cross-channel delivery and multi-screen experiences.

Live Streaming

With its own purpose built live streaming studio and master control room at Fox Studios Australia, Tribal Live provides turnkey solutions for clients looking to live stream multi-camera television style productions across digital channels and social media platforms.

Studio & IP Workflow

Tribal Lives’ studio is conveniently located at Fox Studios Australia. A turnkey solution, the studio is equipped with six HD cameras, LiteGear lighting grid and desk, Roland sound desk and an 8-M/E, 24 channel video switcher with an integrated audio mixer, media players, titles, graphics and DVE effects.

 

Equipped with the latest NDI Live IP Production Workflow, Tribal Live can access, route and switch live HD video over IP and turn any device connected to a LAN into a video source irrespective of where it is in the world.

Pre-Recorded Entertainment

Tribal Live can help clients slash post production budgets and timelines by creating a ‘simulated live environment’ and switching cameras, mixing audio, inserting graphics and cutting in other media in ‘real-time’.

For publishers and high volume content creators, Tribal Live provides them with the resources and pipeline to create premium content, which also breaks stories and reach audiences first.

Branded Live Players

Tribal Live can distribute live streams on customised branded players to clients’ websites, which are mobile responsive and have adaptive multi-bitrate streams for desktop and mobile devices.

Monetisation

Tribal Live can assist clients with the integration of advertising, subscription and transactional video on demand solutions, including the insertion of server side pre, mid and post-roll advertising.

Broadcast Graphics

Tribal Lives’ live production system is equipped with the power to include 3D broadcast graphics; lower thirds and multi-camera virtual and augmented reality sets with support for 3D positioning of talent and live camera movements.

Analytics

Tribal Live provides clients with in-depth data and real-time video analytics of audience behaviour across all devices, destinations and geographic regions.

Connectivity & Live Links

Tribal Live can live stream events when there is no Internet or satellite connection by live linking the event back to Tribal Lives’ master control room. From here, Tribal Live can distribute and syndicate live events to an endless number of channels and platforms simultaneously, including client CDN’s, Facebook, YouTube, YouTube Live, Twitter, Twitch, LinkedIn and UStream.
